## 🎯 WHAT'S NEW IN v2.1.0

### **1. Neuro-Box 6D Validation** ⭐ MAJOR ADDITION
- Maps copy to 6-axis neurochemical activation model
- Each axis = specific neurotransmitter + brainwave frequency
- **BODY** (Dopamine/1.5Hz) → Safety, status elevation
- **HEART** (GABA/1.0Hz) → Emotional stimulation, uniqueness
- **MIND** (Acetylcholine/1.25Hz) → Clarity, logic, proof (CRITICAL)
- **SPIRIT** (Serotonin/0.75Hz) → Social proof, tribal belonging
- **PSYCHE** (Adrenaline/0.5Hz) → Action clarity, CTA
- **CONSCIENCE** (Oxytocin/0.3Hz) → Community, ongoing support

**Balance Check:**
- Vertical (BODY ↔ HEART): Max 4-point diff
- Horizontal (MIND ↔ SPIRIT): Max 3-point diff
- Depth (PSYCHE ↔ CONSCIENCE): Max 3-point diff

**Frequency Cascade Validation:**
- Ideal flow: High → Low Hz (1.5Hz → 0.3Hz)
- Establishes safety first, ends with community

### **2. Purchase Driver Sequence Validation** ⭐ MAJOR ADDITION
- 5 drivers must activate sequentially
- **Driver 1:** HIGHER STATUS (Safety/Dopamine) - "Will this elevate me?"
- **Driver 2:** EMOTIONAL STIMULATION (GABA) - "Will I feel alive?"
- **Driver 3:** STRESS RELIEF/VALIDATION (Acetylcholine) - "Am I smart for choosing this?"
- **Driver 4:** WHAT OTHERS WANT (Serotonin) - "Do people like me want this?"
- **Driver 5:** STEAL (Flow) - "Is this deal so good it feels like stealing?"

**Sequence Violations Detected:**
- Skipped driver (missing entirely)
- Out of sequence (e.g., social proof before safety)
- Premature CTA (before all 5 drivers activated)

---

### **3. Convection Model Integration** ⭐ PHILOSOPHY SHIFT
Replaced "conversion" language with "convection" (earn advocacy, don't manipulate).

**3-Phase Convection:**
1. **ATTENTION** (Earned, not demanded) - Hook quality, no clickbait
2. **APPRECIATION** (Cultivated before asking) - Value delivery upfront
3. **AFFECTION** (Won through integrity) - Shareability, trust-building

**Convection Test (3 Questions):**
- Would they keep reading even if NOT buying?
- Would non-buyers still appreciate this?
- Would they recommend to a friend?

**Pass:** All 3 = YES
**Fail Indicators:** CTA before value, fake urgency, manipulation

**Metrics Reframed:**
- CTR → Attention Rate
- CVR → Convection Rate
- AOV → AVD (Average Value Delivered)

## 🎓 LEAD GEN USE CASE EXAMPLE

**Scenario:** Analyzing prospect's sales page before outreach

**Input:**
```
Prospect: FinTech SaaS company
Page: Their pricing page
Mode: PROSPECT
```

**Output:**
```yaml
strengths_acknowledged:
  - "Your value stack is excellent - the 'Enterprise vs Standard' comparison table makes the choice crystal clear"
  - "Your social proof is strong - the Fortune 500 logos above the fold build immediate credibility"

critical_gaps:
  - gap_1:
      dimension: "SMART (MIND/Acetylcholine)"
      score: "5.5/10"
      evidence: "Mechanism section says 'Our AI-powered platform uses machine learning' but doesn't explain HOW. No proof pillars."
      impact: "20-30% lost convection - prospects don't understand WHAT they're buying"
      fix: "Add 3 proof pillars explaining specific ML capabilities with use cases"

  - gap_2:
      dimension: "Purchase Driver 3 (VALIDATION)"
      score: "MISSING"
      evidence: "No studies, data, or research cited. Claim: 'Increase efficiency by 40%' but no proof."
      impact: "15-25% lost trust - sophisticated buyers need validation"
      fix: "Add case study or third-party audit validating the 40% claim"

pitch_angles:
  - "I noticed your pricing page is converting at [X%]. I specialize in 'clarity optimization' for SaaS companies and spotted a critical gap in your mechanism explanation that's likely costing you 20-30% of potential conversions. Would you be open to a 15-min audit where I'll show you the exact fix?"

estimated_impact: "25-40% convection rate improvement possible"

next_step: "Send personalized Loom video walking through their page with gaps highlighted + fixes demonstrated"
```

**Why This Works:**
1. Acknowledges 2 strengths first (builds credibility)
2. Uses technical language (Neuro-Box, Purchase Drivers) to demonstrate expertise
3. Quotes their copy specifically (shows real analysis, not generic pitch)
4. Quantifies impact (X% improvement)
5. Offers specific next step (Loom audit)
