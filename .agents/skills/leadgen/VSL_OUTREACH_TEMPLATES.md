## LOOM SCRIPT

### Opening (0:00-0:30)
```
"Hey [NAME], [YOUR NAME] here.

I watched your [TRAINING NAME] and wanted to share 
a few quick observations that could help your 
conversions.

This isn't a pitch — just things I noticed that 
you might find useful. Let me share my screen..."
```

### Weakness 1 — Hook (0:30-2:00)
```
"So right here at the beginning... [play their VSL]

Your opening line is '[THEIR LINE]'. It's decent, 
but it's not creating a strong curiosity gap.

Viewers are deciding in these first 45 seconds 
whether to keep watching or bounce.

What I'd suggest: [SPECIFIC FIX]

This alone can improve retention by 10-15%."
```

### Weakness 2 — Proof (2:00-3:30)
```
"Now let me skip ahead to minute [X]... [play VSL]

This is your strongest testimonial. The problem? 
It's too late. By minute [X], viewers have already 
built up skepticism.

Moving this to minute 5-7 — before they start 
doubting — typically lifts completion rates 15-20%.

[Show where it should go]"
```

### Weakness 3 — CTA (3:30-5:00)
```
"Last one — your call to action at the end...

'[THEIR CTA]' — there's no urgency here. No reason 
to act today versus next week.

What works better: [SPECIFIC FIX]

Add scarcity, add a clear next step, add a reason 
to act NOW."
```

### Close (5:00-5:30)
```
"So those are the 3 quick wins:
1. Sharper hook
2. Earlier proof
3. Stronger CTA

Implementing these typically lifts conversions 
15-25%. If you want help with any of this, happy 
to chat. Either way, hope this was useful.

Talk soon."
```

## DO NOT SAY

❌ "I'm reaching out because..."  
❌ "I hope this email finds you well..."  
❌ "I'd love to pick your brain..."  
❌ "I'm a huge fan of your work..."  
❌ "I think we could really help you..."  
❌ "Would you be open to a quick call?"  

## DO SAY

✅ Specific observations about THEIR content  
✅ Timestamps and exact quotes  
✅ Concrete fixes, not vague suggestions  
✅ "No pitch, just the analysis"  
✅ "Worth a look?"  
✅ Direct, no fluff
