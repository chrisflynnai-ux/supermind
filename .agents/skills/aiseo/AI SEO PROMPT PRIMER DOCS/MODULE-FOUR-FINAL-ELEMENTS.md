## Case Study 2: E-Commerce (Outdoor Gear) - "Best Tents for Tall People" (Condensed)

**Context:** Online retailer struggling against Amazon. Generic tent reviews weren't converting.

**Challenge:** "Our product reviews got views but zero sales. Amazon dominated every 'best tent' search."

**Strategy:** Niche qualification + personal experience hook
- Hook: "I'm 6'5" and slept in 12 tents. Only 3 didn't make me feel like a sardine." (EVR: 9/10)
- Qualification filter: "If you're 6'2" or taller..."
- Archetype: Ranking/Rating + Personal Story
- Native CTA: Affiliate links + "Use code TALLCAMPER for 15% off"

**Execution Timeline:**
- Week 1: Bought and tested 12 tents (actual footage of cramped experiences)
- Week 2: Filmed body-first (showing each tent setup, highlighting space issues)
- Week 3: Created comparison table (height clearance, vestibule space, packed size)
- Week 4: Published with Amazon affiliate links + direct store links

**Results (90 days):**
- Views: 284,000 (67.6x their average)
- CTR: 9.4%
- AVD: 61%
- Affiliate revenue: $31,420 (Amazon Associates)
- Direct store sales: $15,900
- **Total revenue: $47,320**
- Email subscribers from video: 2,847 (pop-up for "Tall Campers Gear Guide")

**Key Success Factor:** Hyper-niche qualification created "I've finally found someone like me!" resonance.

**Downstream Impact:**
- Module 2: Discovered 127 new "for tall people" modifiers across categories
- Module 5: "Ultimate Tall Camper Gear List" lead magnet (34% conversion)
- Module 6: Created "Tall People Outdoor Gear" category hub

## Case Study 3: B2B Consulting (Marketing Agency) - "How to Sell SEO Retainers" (Condensed)

**Context:** Small agency (DR 12) couldn't rank articles. Needed credibility fast.

**Challenge:** "We had expertise but zero Domain Authority. Text content was invisible."

**Strategy:** Authority-first video with named framework
- Hook: "I've closed $840K in SEO retainers using a 4-step framework big agencies don't teach."
- Archetype: Behind-the-Scenes + Tutorial
- Framework: "The ACES Framework" (Audit, Case, Expectations, Scope)
- Proof: Showed actual sales call recording (client details blurred)
- CTA: Free "ACES Framework Template" download

**Execution Timeline:**
- Week 1: Outlined ACES Framework (displayed on screen with graphics)
- Week 2: Filmed real sales call (got client permission), edited for teaching moments
- Week 3: Created framework template as downloadable PDF
- Week 4: Published + posted to LinkedIn with video clip

**Results (180 days):**
- Views: 31,400 (174x their average)
- CTR: 11.2% (controversial hook worked)
- AVD: 67% (highest in their channel history)
- Template downloads: 1,247
- Qualified leads (from template download): 34
- **Closed deals: 7 clients at avg $12K = $84,000 revenue**
- LinkedIn shares: 847 (video went mini-viral in marketing community)

**Key Success Factor:** Named framework ("ACES") became citeable—Perplexity/ChatGPT referenced it 23 times in 6 months.

**AI Citation Proof:**
- Perplexity query: "How do agencies sell SEO retainers?"
- Response: "One effective approach is the ACES Framework (Audit, Case, Expectations, Scope)..."
- Citation: [Video URL]

**Downstream Impact:**
- Module 1: Validated video-first strategy for low-DR sites
- Module 3: Blog post embedding video ranked #3 (video authority boosted page)
- Module 7: ACES Framework became their proprietary audit methodology

    category: "hook"

      Hook 1 (Visual): Thumbnail image/text
      Hook 2 (Text): Video title
