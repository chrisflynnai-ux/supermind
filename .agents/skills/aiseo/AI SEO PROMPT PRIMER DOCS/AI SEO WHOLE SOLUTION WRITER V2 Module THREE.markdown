3. Knowledge Blocks (Deep Dive Frameworks)
KB-WSA-ARCHITECTURE-v1.0 (6 Pages)
1. The WSA Philosophy: Authority Over Volume
• Concept: A Whole Solution Article (WSA) is not a blog post. It is a comprehensive asset designed to be the definitive answer to a specific problem. It renders competitor content obsolete by covering the "Why," "How," "Tools," and "Pitfalls" in one place.
• The "Answer Every Question" Principle: A WSA must anticipate the user's next 3 questions and answer them immediately (Query Fan-out). It does not force the user to go back to Google.
• Information Gain: Google patents cite "Information Gain" as a ranking factor. Your WSA must provide new information (a new angle, unique data point, or framework) that does not exist in the corpus.
2. The 7-Section Architecture (Detailed)
• Section 1: Safety Anchor (BODY/Dopamine - 1.5 Hz):
    ? Goal: Establish trust immediately. Calms the "fight or flight" response to sales.
    ? Tactics: Use diagnostic language ("I'm not here to sell you"). Display credentials early. Mirror the user's pain accurately ("If you're tired of X...").
• Section 2: Transformation Hook (HEART/GABA - 0.5 Hz):
    ? Goal: Trigger emotional desire and curiosity.
    ? Tactics: Paint the "After" state. Use sensory language. "Imagine if you never had to worry about [Pain] again."
• Section 3: Mechanism Explained (MIND/Acetylcholine - 14 Hz):
    ? Goal: Satisfy the logical brain. Explain why the old way failed and why this new way works.
    ? Tactics: Introduce a "Named Framework" (Citation Magnet). Use data tables. Numbered steps.
• Section 4: Proof Layer (SPIRIT/Serotonin - 10 Hz):
    ? Goal: Social validation and status.
    ? Tactics: "Here are 3 others who achieved this." Use screenshots, not just text.
• Section 5: Action Path (PSYCHE/Adrenaline - 30 Hz):
    ? Goal: Create frictionless action.
    ? Tactics: Step-by-step protocol. "Do this first." Downloadable checklist.
• Section 6: Community Promise (CONSCIENCE/Oxytocin - 0.3 Hz):
    ? Goal: Belonging and support.
    ? Tactics: "You are not alone. Join the tribe." "We support you."
• Section 7: Value Stack (The "Steal" - All Axes):
    ? Goal: Make the offer irresistible.
    ? Tactics: "Get the tool + the course + the template for $X." Price anchoring.
3. BOFU Content Types & Structures
• Comparison Pages ("X vs Y"):
    ? Structure: Intro (I tested both) -> Feature Matrix (Table) -> Deep Dive X -> Deep Dive Y -> Unique "Winner" Verdict based on use case.
    ? Neuro Focus: SMART (Clarity) + SIGNIFICANT (Social Proof).
• "Best Of" Collections:
    ? Structure: Criteria for selection -> Comparison Table -> Individual Reviews -> "Best For" tags.
    ? Neuro Focus: SAFE (Trust) + STEAL (Value).
• Problem-Solution ("How to Fix X"):
    ? Structure: Diagnosis (It's not your fault) -> Root Cause -> The Solution (Framework) -> Protocol -> Prevention.
    ? Neuro Focus: SPECIAL (Unique angle) + SUPPORTED (Clear steps).
4. Schema & AI Citation Optimization
• FAQ Schema: Use JSON-LD to markup 5-8 questions/answers. AI agents prioritize this for "Direct Answers."
• HTML Tables: The #1 most cited element in AI Overviews. Use <table> tags for pricing, features, or pros/cons. Do not use images of tables.
• Entity Definitions: Use Semantic Triples (Subject-Predicate-Object) in the first paragraph. "Rankability is an SEO tool that tracks..."
5. Internal Linking Architecture
• Hub-and-Spoke Integration: WSAs often serve as "Hubs" or primary "Spokes" in the Module 1 cluster map.
• Contextual Bridges: Anchor text must be descriptive. Instead of "Click here," use "Learn more about the 5-step technical audit."
• Authority Flow: Spoke pages link up to the Hub. Hub pages link to the Money Page (Conversion).

KB-NEURO-RESONANCE-WRITING-v1.0 (6 Pages)
1. The Corrected Neuro-Box 6D Model
• BOTTOM (BODY/Dopamine): Safety, Status, Resource Acquisition. Trigger: "Am I safe? Will this elevate me?"
• TOP (HEART/GABA): Emotion, Connection, Calm. Trigger: "Is this unique? Will I feel alive?"
• LEFT (MIND/Acetylcholine): Logic, Focus, Validation. Trigger: "Does this make sense? Am I smart?"
• RIGHT (SPIRIT/Serotonin): Significance, Dominance, Proof. Trigger: "Do others want this? Does it matter?"
• FRONT (PSYCHE/Adrenaline): Action, Urgency, Fight/Flight. Trigger: "Can I act NOW? Is the path clear?"
• BACK (CONSCIENCE/Oxytocin): Belonging, Support, Obligation. Trigger: "Am I supported? Do I belong?"
2. The 5 Purchase Driver Sequence
• Sequence Rule: You cannot skip steps. The brain must process them in order.
    1. Higher Status (Dopamine): "This makes you better/safer." (Intro)
    2. Emotional Stimulation (GABA): "This feels good/exciting." (Hook)
    3. Stress Relief/Validation (Acetylcholine): "This solves the confusion." (Mechanism)
    4. Social Validation (Serotonin): "Others are winning with this." (Proof)
    5. The "Steal" (All Axes): "The value outweighs the cost." (Offer/CTA)
3. The 7S Chain Validation Framework
• SAFE (Target ?8.0): Guarantee, credentials, transparency. "You're not alone."
• SPECIAL (Target ?7.0): Unique angle, emotional language. "Not for everyone."
• SMART (Target ?8.0): Clarity is everything. Mechanism, logic, data.
• SIGNIFICANT (Target ?7.0): Testimonials, case studies. "Join thousands."
• SUPPORTED (Target ?7.0): Clear CTA, path forward, friction removal.
• SUPERIOR (Target ?6.0): Community, belonging, support.
• STEAL (Target ?7.0): Value stack, price anchoring, bonuses.
4. Balance Checking
• Excitatory vs. Inhibitory: You must balance "Hype" (Excitatory) with "Calm/Safety" (Inhibitory).
• Vertical Balance: BODY (Status) vs HEART (Empathy). Max diff 4 points.
• Horizontal Balance: MIND (Logic) vs SPIRIT (Social Proof). Max diff 3 points.
• Depth Balance: PSYCHE (Urgency) vs CONSCIENCE (Support). Max diff 3 points.
• Imbalance Consequence: High Urgency without Support = Scam vibe. High Logic without Emotion = Boring/No Action.
5. Trauma-Aware Writing (Blocked Axes)
• Body Blocked: "I've been scammed." Fix: Transparency, guarantees.
• Heart Blocked: "I don't deserve this." Fix: Permission language ("It's not your fault").
• Mind Blocked: "Too complicated." Fix: Simplify, numbered steps.
• Spirit Blocked: "Experts lie." Fix: Third-party proof, screenshots.

KB-HUMAN-VOICE-POLISH-v1.0 (5 Pages)
1. The 6-Pass Velvet Hammer Procedure
• Pass 1: AI Detox. Search and destroy banned words: "landscape," "unlock," "dive in," "tapestry," "elevate," "realm," "game-changer."
• Pass 2: Cardiac Rhythm. Vary sentence length to create a "heartbeat." Short. Short. Long flowing explanation. Fragment. No 3 consecutive same-length sentences.
• Pass 3: Sensory Injection. Replace abstract words with felt senses. "Fast" -> "Blink and you miss it." "Hard" -> "Grinding uphill."
• Pass 4: Open Loop Architect. End paragraphs with curiosity hooks. "But here's the catch..." "And that's when it fell apart."
• Pass 5: "You" Scan. Ensure the word "You" appears 3x more than "I" or "We." Flip "We provide X" to "You get X."
• Pass 6: 7S Audit. Score the draft against the 7S framework. Strengthen weak dimensions.
2. The Convection Model (Not Conversion)
• Concept: Don't "Convert" (extract). Create "Convection" (heat that rises naturally).
• Attention (Earned): Do not demand attention with clickbait. Earn it with value in the first sentence.
• Appreciation (Cultivated): Teach them something valuable before the pitch. Establish reciprocity.
• Affection (Won): Create a bond through vulnerability, shared values, and community.
3. Voice DNA (G.I.V.E. Framework)
• Gratify: Give the answer immediately (first 2 sentences). No throat-clearing.
• Inspire: Show the "After" state clearly. "Imagine if..."
• Validate: Acknowledge their pain is real. "You're not crazy for thinking this."
• Enhance: Leave them smarter than they arrived. Provide a framework/model.
4. Quality Validation Checklist
• Bar Test: Would I say this to a friend at a bar?
• Smart 15-Year-Old Test: Is it clear enough for a smart teenager?
• Confidence: No hedging ("I think," "maybe"). Definitive claims.
• Utility: Does it solve the problem?
