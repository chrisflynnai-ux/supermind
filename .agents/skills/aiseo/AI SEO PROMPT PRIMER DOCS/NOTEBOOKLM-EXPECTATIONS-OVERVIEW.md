## KEY PRINCIPLES (How to Approach This)

### **1. Deep Solutions Authority (DSA) Standard**
Don't create summaries or listicles. Create COMPLETE solutions.

**Test**: Does each section answer "What is the full solution and why does it work?"

**Example**:
- ❌ Bad: "Use BOFU queries to rank better"
- ✅ Good: "BOFU queries (bottom-of-funnel, commercial intent like 'X vs Y' or 'best X for Y') rank with less authority because AI Mode cannot complete transactions, so users still click through. Here's the complete protocol: Step 1 [detailed]..."

### **2. Evidence-Based Claims (Proof Discipline)**
Every claim must be:
- Cited to primary source (Google announcement, research study), OR
- Labeled as expert analysis with caveat, OR
- Marked as hypothesis/prediction

**Never**:
- ❌ "This will double your traffic" (guaranteed outcome)
- ❌ "Studies show..." (vague, no citation)
- ❌ "Experts say..." (no attribution)

**Always**:
- ✅ "Google announced AI Mode on May 20, 2025 (Source: blog.google)"
- ✅ "Our hypothesis based on current trends: AI Mode will become default by Q4 2026 (not confirmed)"
- ✅ "In our test of 25 LinkedIn articles, 18 ranked page 1 within 7 days (your results may vary)"

### **3. Market-Language Accuracy**
Use REAL customer words from Reddit, forums, reviews.

**Example**:
- ❌ Marketer jargon: "Optimize your content funnel for maximum conversion efficiency"
- ✅ Customer language: "I can't track traffic from ChatGPT and my boss is asking why we're still investing in SEO"

### **4. Actionable Specificity**
Every recommendation must have:
- Exact action (not vague "improve content")
- Tool needed (specific name, not category)
- Time estimate (realistic)
- Success metric (how to know it worked)

**Example**:
- ❌ Vague: "Create better content"
- ✅ Specific: "Use Module 3 (whole-solution-writer) to create a 2,000-word WSA targeting 'Salesforce vs HubSpot for 50-person teams'. Time: 4-6 hours. Success: Rank top 10 within 90 days, generate 3-5 SQLs/month."

### **5. Constitutional Compliance (ULTRAMIND v2.0)**
This system must:
- Provide intelligent frameworks (not rigid templates)
- Separate strategy → orchestration → execution
- Avoid manipulation (no fake scarcity, no overclaiming)
- Maintain ethical execution

## STRATEGIC CONSTRAINTS (What NOT to Include)

Based on the strategic guidance, AVOID recommending:

❌ **Reddit Parasite SEO** - Unsustainable, spam saturation expected through 2026
❌ **Link Buying** - Google will stop counting them, causing sudden ranking drops
❌ **AI-Generated Listicles** - Commoditized, low trust, filtered by Google
❌ **Separate LLM Optimization** - Wrong model; AI Mode pulls from traditional top 10 rankings
❌ **Top-of-Funnel (TOFU) Focus** - AI Mode absorbs informational queries; prioritize BOFU instead
❌ **Perfect Attribution Tracking** - Broken in AI Mode era; use bespoke analytics, cohort tracking

**Strategic Priorities** (What TO Include):

✅ **BOFU Query Targeting** - High-intent, commercial queries AI can't satisfy without clicks
✅ **Topical Authority Building** - Comprehensive coverage of topic clusters
✅ **Citation Optimization** - Structured data, unique data, cite-able proof
✅ **Multi-Platform Presence** - YouTube, LinkedIn, owned properties
✅ **Bespoke Analytics** - Track citations, BOFU conversions, cohort behavior (not just traffic)
