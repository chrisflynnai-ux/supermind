#### KB-MMA-PRIORITIZATION-v1.0 (4-5 pages)

**Page 1: The MMA Scoring Framework Explained**
- Money (1-10): Does this query indicate budget?
  * 1-3: Informational ("What is X?") - User researching, no wallet
  * 4-6: Consideration ("Best X") - User comparing, wallet nearby
  * 7-10: Purchase intent ("Pricing," "vs [product]") - Wallet out, ready to buy

- Movement (1-10): How fast can we rank?
  * 1-3: Dominated by huge brands (Ahrefs, HubSpot DR 90+)
  * 4-6: Mixed results (opportunity for authority sites DR 40-70)
  * 7-10: Low competition (new query, niche long-tail, DR 20+ can win)

- Authority (1-10): Does ranking here build topical power?
  * 1-3: Tangentially related (avoid unless Money score is 9+)
  * 4-6: Related but not core (nice-to-have for cluster depth)
  * 7-10: Core to brand positioning (must-rank for authority)

**Page 2: How to Calculate Total MMA Score**
- Formula: `(Money × 2) + Movement + Authority = Total (Max 50)`
- Why Money is weighted 2x: Revenue > Speed > Authority
- Example calculations:
  * "AI SEO tools for SaaS": (9×2) + 7 + 9 = 34/50 (HIGH PRIORITY)
  * "What is SEO": (2×2) + 1 + 3 = 8/50 (DELETE)
  * "Best CRM": (7×2) + 3 + 6 = 23/50 (MEDIUM - High money but hard to rank)

**Page 3: The "Quick Win" Filter**
- Definition: Movement ≥7, Money ≥5
- Why target these first: Low-hanging fruit for revenue
- Examples from transcripts:
  * "Technical SEO audit for SaaS" (Money 8, Movement 8, Authority 7) = 31/50
  * "Affordable CRM for small business" (Money 7, Movement 9, Authority 5) = 32/50
- Strategy: Build momentum with 5-10 quick wins in first 90 days

**Page 4: The "Authority Play" Strategy**

### 3. EXPAND FAILURE MODES (Add 2-3 pages)

**Current**: 3 brief failure modes
**Required**: 5-6 detailed modes with diagnostics and recovery

**Example Full Failure Mode:**

Failure Mode 4: The "Zero-Volume" Panic

Symptom:
• Avoiding long-tail keywords because Ahrefs shows "0-10 volume"
• Only targeting keywords with 500+ searches/month
• Missing niche, high-intent queries that actually convert

Root Cause:
• Misunderstanding how keyword tools work
• Tools underreport long-tail and emerging queries
• Volume ≠ Value (200 high-intent visits > 20,000 informational)

Diagnostic Questions:
1. What % of your target keywords have <100 monthly searches?
   - If <10%: You're ignoring the long tail (likely missing conversions)
2. Do your highest-converting pages target high-volume keywords?
   - Often conversion leaders have "0-10 volume" in tools
3. Are you targeting "compact keywords" (4+ words)?
   - Long-tail specificity = higher conversion intent

Real-World Example:
• SaaS company ignored "SaaS SEO agency for B2B fintech" (Ahrefs: 0 volume)
• Competitor created page, got 15 visits/month, 3 converted to $10k deals
• Revenue from "zero volume" keyword: $30k/month
• Lesson: Tools lag reality; trust pain research over volume data

Step-by-Step Recovery:
1. Audit current keyword list for long-tail exclusions
2. Use Reddit/Quora to find "zero volume" pain-driven queries
3. Create 5 test pages targeting specific long-tail queries
4. Track conversions (not just traffic) for 90 days
5. If conversion rate >2%, expand long-tail strategy
6. Build "Topic Cluster" around long-tail winners

Prevention:
• Use MMA Framework (Money score matters more than volume)
• Validate every "0 volume" keyword against Reddit search
• Track conversions per keyword, not just rankings

Tools:
• Reddit search, Quora, answer forums (find real language)
• GA4 goal tracking (measure conversions, not traffic)
• Long-tail keyword generator (use ChatGPT/Perplexity)

Timeline to Recovery:
• Week 1-2: Identify long-tail opportunities from forums
• Week 3-6: Create 5 test pages
• Month 3: Evaluate conversion data, scale winners
